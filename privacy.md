# Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Virtual Flight Data Systems (vFDS), vfds.dev, and the vEDST application ("we", "us", or "our") handle information when you use our software.

## Overview
We are committed to protecting your privacy. vFDS does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user information to our servers.

## Information We Do Not Collect
vFDS does not collect or store:
- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics or telemetry
- Browsing history or activity logs
- Location data beyond what you configure in the application
- Any data on external servers operated by us

## Local Data Storage
vEDST stores application settings and preferences locally within your browser. This data:
- Remains entirely on your machine
- Is never transmitted to any external servers
- Can be deleted by clearing your browser's local storage for the vEDST application

## vNAS Integration
vEDST integrates directly with your existing vNAS session and does not create separate data collection. Please refer to [vNAS](https://vnas.vatsim.net/) for their data handling practices.

## Children's Privacy
vFDS does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
